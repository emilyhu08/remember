Privacy Policy

Remember - Smart Recall

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

Remember - Smart Recall ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Notes and Reminders: All notes, content, and reminder settings you create
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications and app behavior
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (stored locally only)

Data We Collect

  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device, used for push notifications
  • Push Token: Required to send you reminder notifications
  • Optional Cloud Sync Data: If you choose to enable cloud sync, your notes and settings are securely stored in our database

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Notifications: To send you reminder notifications at the times you set
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): To sync your notes across devices if you enable this feature
  • App Improvement: To understand how the app is used and improve functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • Local Storage: Notes and reminders are primarily stored on your device using secure local storage
  • Cloud Storage: Optional cloud sync uses Supabase with industry-standard encryption
  • No Third-Party Access: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For optional cloud sync and data storage. See Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For sending reminder notifications

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your notes and data at any time within the app
  • Export: You can export your data (feature coming soon)
  • Opt-Out: You can disable cloud sync and notifications at any time in settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require an account or personal information to use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, all local data is removed. If you use cloud sync, you can request data deletion by contacting us.
