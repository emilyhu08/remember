Remember - Smart Recall

About Remember - Smart Recall

Remember - Smart Recall is an intelligent note and reminder app that uses proven memory science to help you retain information longer. Our unique forgetting curve feature schedules reminders at optimal intervals for maximum memory retention.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the forgetting curve feature?
The forgetting curve is based on the Ebbinghaus memory retention principle. When you enable this feature for a note, the app automatically schedules reminders at scientifically-optimized intervals to help you remember the information long-term. This is perfect for studying, learning new skills, or retaining important information.
How do I create a reminder?
  1. Tap the "+" button to create a new note
  2. Add your title and content
  3. Toggle "Use forgetting curve" for science-based reminders, or set a custom schedule
  4. Choose your reminder time
  5. Save your note
Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure you've granted notification permissions to the app:
  1. Go to Settings → Notifications → Remember
  2. Enable "Allow Notifications"
  3. Make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb mode
Is my data private and secure?
Yes! Your notes are stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Can I sync my notes across devices?
Cloud sync is available and can be enabled in the app settings. This allows you to access your notes and reminders across all your devices.
How do I delete a note?
Swipe left on any note in the list and tap the delete button. You can also delete a note from within the note editing screen.
Can I export my notes?
Export functionality is coming in a future update! For now, your notes are safely stored locally and in the cloud (if sync is enabled).
